What is it?

These 3 strains are: Sativa, Indica, and Hybrid. The 3 cannabis strains that embody different strengths, that affect body and mind.


SATIVA STRAIN

  • Discovered by biologist Carl Linnaeus, who identified the strain (sativa) as a unique species.

  • Known to have higher CBD content over THC content.

    • Physiological attributes are:

  • Tall thin plant, leaves are slender in shape

  • Can grow as tall as 20 feet.

  • Thrives in warm climates around the equator - Effects: energetic, creative, etc.


    • INDICA STRAIN

  • Discovered by Jean-Baptiste Lamarck 1785 in India.

  • Known to have higher THC than CBD contents.

    • Physiological attributes are:

  • Short bushy plant. Leaves are thick.

  • Can be found in high altitudes with colder climates.

  • Thrives in warm climates around the equator - Effects: sleepy, relaxed, etc.
